Fish Wife's Sardines with Preserved Lemon

Fish Wife's Sardines with Preserved Lemon are a zesty and vibrant option for those who love bold flavors. Packed in olive oil with preserved lemon, these sardines offer a tangy, citrusy twist on a classic favorite.

Perfect for adding a burst of flavor to salads, pasta dishes, or enjoying straight from the tin with some crackers. The preserved lemon adds a unique depth of flavor that complements the rich, tender sardines.

For an added treat, pair them with a glass of chilled white wine for the ultimate Mediterranean-inspired experience!
