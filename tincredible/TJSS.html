Trader Joe's Lightly Smoked Salmon

Trader Joe's Farm Raised Lightly Smoked Salmon offers a delicious and slightly smoky flavor that pairs perfectly with crackers, bagels, or salads. This premium smoked salmon is a versatile addition to any meal.

With a rich, tender texture and a subtle smoky finish, this salmon is perfect for breakfast or a light snack. It's also a great topping for your favorite pasta or on top of a salad, adding both flavor and protein to your dish.

Enjoy it as part of a charcuterie board or simply with a squeeze of lemon for a refreshing, satisfying bite.
