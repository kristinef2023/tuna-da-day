The Classic Tuna Melts

Three Decker Diner: $13.75

Served with your choice of potato salad or shoestrings, this is the best bang for your buck tuna melt in the city!


B&H Dairy: $12

Challah! Toasted and griddled, have it with a pickle or make it a combo and get a soup!


S&P Lunch: $17

Slight price jump.. however you are instantly transported into the heart of New York City when you enter. The griddled rye is certainly what you pay and return for!


The Flame Diner: $19

Open face, but we recommend you stack 'em! Bit pricey for the amount of fries and quality, but at this diner, you can get tuna 24/7!


The Commodore: $14

Don't dally on this dilly melt.. melted with gruyere on rye this is our current favorite melt!


Edith's Sandwich Counter: $15

Nothin' but smiles here! This locally owned melt doesn't just have American and Emmenthal, they also have us cheesin' ear to ear!
